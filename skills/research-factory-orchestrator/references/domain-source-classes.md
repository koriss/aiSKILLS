
# Domain Source Classes

The pipeline is identical for every topic. Source classes change by domain.

These are examples, not hardcoded limits.

## Games

- official site;
- developer posts;
- patch notes;
- storefronts;
- reviews;
- player statistics;
- interviews;
- technical benchmarks;
- community claims treated as weak/contextual.

## Politics

- laws;
- official statements;
- voting records;
- budgets;
- court/regulatory documents;
- public datasets;
- reputable reporting;
- opposition/criticism sources.

## Biology / science reference

- textbooks;
- review papers;
- primary literature;
- university sources;
- PubMed/NCBI/WHO/CDC style sources where relevant;
- datasets;
- diagrams and tables with page/table anchors.

## Business / market

- filings;
- registries;
- investor materials;
- procurement;
- pricing pages;
- market datasets;
- specialist publications.

## History

- primary documents;
- archives;
- scholarly works;
- chronologies;
- contemporary records.
