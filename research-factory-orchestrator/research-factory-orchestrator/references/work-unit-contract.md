
# Work Unit Contract

Required fields:

- work_unit_id;
- objective;
- entity_scope;
- coverage_categories;
- int_families/source_families;
- required_inputs;
- required_outputs;
- forbidden_completion;
- validators;
- max_attempts;
- retry_policy;
- merge_target;
- done_definition.

A work unit cannot reduce its own scope.
