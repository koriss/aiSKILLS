SECTION 1: NLP FOUNDATIONAL TEXTS (Neuro-Linguistic Programming)
================================================================================

BOOK 1: "Frogs into Princes" (aka "Frogs into Princes: The 1991 NLP Conference")
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Richard Bandler, John Grinder
Year: 1979 (original), 1991 (conference edition)
Description: Foundational NLP text from the early Sacramento workshops. Contains
core reframing techniques and modeling excellence. One of the first widely
available NLP books.
Where to find: https://www.academia.edu/34258965/Frogs_into_Princes_NLP_
Notes: Full PDF available on Academia.edu

BOOK 2: "The Structure of Magic" (Vol. I & II)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Richard Bandler, John Grinder
Year: 1975
Description: Foundational NLP text on communication and therapy. Introduced the
Meta Model - a set of language patterns for uncovering unconscious distortions
and deletions in speech.
Where to find: https://www.ayanetwork.com/upload/nlp/The%20Structure%20Of%20Magic%20Vols%20I%20And%20II%20By%20Richard%20Bandler%20And%20John%20Grinder%20%5Bfound%20via%20www.FileDonkey.com%5D.pdf

BOOK 3: "Trance-Formations: Neuro-Linguistic Programming and the Structure of Real People"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: John Grinder, Richard Bandler
Year: 1981
Description: Advanced NLP techniques for personal transformation. Explores
modeling human behavior and creating lasting change through trancework.
Where to find: https://www.scribd.com/document/486454240/John-Grinder-Richard-Bandler-Trance-Formations-Neuro-Linguistic-Programming-and-the-Structure-of-Real-People-Pr-1981-pdf

BOOK 4: "Reframing: Neuro-Linguistic Programming and the Transformation of Meaning"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Richard Bandler, John Grinder
Year: 1982
Description: NLP technique for changing the meaning of experiences. Teaches how
to reshape perception by changing the frame around events.
Where to find: https://lifechangework.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/richard-bandler-john-grinder-reframing.pdf

BOOK 5: "Get The Life You Want"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Richard Bandler
Year: 2008
Description: Practical guide to using NLP for rapid personal change. Bandler's
updated techniques for reprogramming behaviors and beliefs.
Where to find: https://sciphilconf.berkeley.edu/filedownload.ashx/mL7131/603317/Richard%20Bandler%20Get%20The%20Life%20You%20Want.pdf

BOOK 6: "Neuro Linguistic Programming Vol 1" (Spanish/English)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Richard Bandler, John Grinder
Description: Spanish language edition of foundational NLP work.
Where to find: https://app.pulsar.uba.ar/fetch.php/libweb/T67742/Richard_Bandler_John_Grinder_Programacion_Neurolinguistica_Vol_1.pdf

BOOK 7: "NLP And The Use Of Language"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: John Grinder, Richard Bandler (via archive.org)
Description: From archive.org collections - covers NLP communication techniques.
Where to find: https://archive.org/download/nlp-work-book/NLP%20And%20The%20Use%20Of%20Language.pdf

SECTION 2: COERCIVE PERSUASION & BRAINWASHING RESEARCH
================================================================================

BOOK 8: "Thought Reform and the Psychology of Totalism: A Study of 'Brainwashing' in China"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Robert Jay Lifton
Year: 1961
Description: CLINICAL FOUNDATIONAL TEXT. Lifton interviewed 25 former Chinese
thought reform subjects. Identified 8 criteria of thought reform: milieu control,
神秘教诲 (mystical manipulation), demand for purity, confession culture,
trustworthiness, loaded language, doctrine over person, and dispensing of existence.
This is the definitive clinical study of coercive persuasion.
Where to find: https://ia601405.us.archive.org/18/items/ThoughtReformAndThePsychologyOfTotalism/Thought_Reform_and_the_Psychology_of_Totalism.pdf
Publisher: W.W. Norton / UNC Press
ISBN: 0393002217

BOOK 9: "Cults in Our Midst: The Continuing Fight Against Their Hidden Menace"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Margaret Thaler Singer, Janja Lalich, with Robert Jay Lifton (forward)
Year: 1995 (revised 1996)
Description: Comprehensive study of cult recruitment and retention methods.
Singer was the leading expert on coercive persuasion in cult contexts.
Covers the same 8 criteria as Lifton, applied to modern cults. Essential
clinical text on thought reform techniques.
Where to find: https://pearl-hifi.com/11_Spirited_Growth/10_Health_Pos/01_Worthy_Authors/Cults_in_Our_Midst__The_Continuing_Fight_Against_Their_Hidden_Menace.pdf
Publisher: Jossey-Bass

BOOK 10: "Combatting Cult Mind Control: The #1 Best-selling Guide to Protection, Rescue, and Recovery from Destructive Cults"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Steven Hassan
Year: 1988 (updated editions)
Description: Ex-cult member turned therapist. Introduces BITE model (Behavior,
Information, Thought, Emotional control). Practical guide for identifying
and recovering from cult control.
Where to find: Various platforms - search "Steven Hassan Combatting Cult Mind Control"

SECTION 3: INFLUENCE & PERSUASION PSYCHOLOGY
================================================================================

BOOK 11: "Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Robert B. Cialdini, Ph.D.
Year: 1984 (new expanded edition 2006)
Description: DEFINITIVE TEXT. Cialdini's 6 (later 7) principles of influence:
1) Reciprocity - we repay what we receive
2) Commitment/Consistency - we honor commitments
3) Social Proof - we look to others for guidance
4) Authority - we defer to experts
5) Liking - we say yes to people we like
6) Scarcity - we want what is rare
7) Unity (added in newer edition) - we trust what aligns with our identity
Where to find (PDF): 
- https://ia800203.us.archive.org/33/items/ThePsychologyOfPersuasion/The%20Psychology%20of%20Persuasion.pdf
- https://github.com/big842/Sham-book/blob/master/Book%20demo/Influent%20-%20The%20psychology%20of%20Persuasion%20-%20Robert%20B.Cialdini.pdf
- https://www.academia.edu/9978388/Influence_The_Psychology_of_Persuasion

BOOK 12: "Contagious: Why Things Catch On"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Jonah Berger, Ph.D. (Wharton)
Year: 2013
Description: The STEPPS framework for viral content:
- Social Currency - makes people look good
- Triggers - top of mind, tip of tongue
- Emotion - high arousal emotions spread
- Public - observable behavior spreads
- Practical Value - useful information spreads
- Stories - wrapped in narrative
Where to find (PDF):
- https://www.academia.edu/41343654/Contagious_why_things_catch_on_jonah_berger
- https://wbl.wharton.upenn.edu/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Contagious-Why-Things-Catch-On.pdf
- https://wordofmouth.org/download/Berger_Contagious.pdf

BOOK 13: "How to Win Friends and Influence People"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Dale Carnegie
Year: 1936
Description: Classic persuasion text. Key techniques:
- Don't criticize, condemn, or complain
- Give honest appreciation
- Arouse in others a WANT
- Smile
- Remember names
- Be a good listener
- Talk in terms of the other person's interest
- Make the other person feel important
- Appeal to nobler motives
Where to find (PDF): https://dn720004.ca.archive.org/0/items/english-collections-1/How%20To%20Win%20Friends%20And%20Influence%20People%20-%20Carnegie%2C%20Dale.pdf

SECTION 4: SOCIAL ENGINEERING & MANIPULATION MANUALS
================================================================================

BOOK 14: "The Art of Deception: Controlling the Human Element of Security"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Kevin Mitnick, William L. Simon
Year: 2002
Description: FOUNDATIONAL SOCIAL ENGINEERING TEXT. Real-world case studies of
human manipulation for information security. Shows how attackers use:
- Pretexting (creating false scenarios)
- Phishing and vishing
- Tailgating and piggybacking
- Elicitation techniques
- Reverse social engineering
Where to find (PDF):
- https://repo.zenk-security.com/Magazine%20E-book/Kevin_Mitnick_-_The_Art_of_Intrusion.pdf (actually "Art of Intrusion" - related)
- https://ia800102.us.archive.org/4/items/pdfsandebooks/UP/Life%20Skills/Teach%20Yourself%20-%20Body%20Language%20%20Ebooks%20-%20Mantesh/The%20Art%20of%20Deception%20-%20Kevin%20Mitnick.pdf
- https://www.scribd.com/document/965090238/Mitnick-The-Art-of-Deception

BOOK 15: "The Art of Intrusion: Real Stories Behind the Exploits of Hackers, Intruders & Deceivers"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Kevin Mitnick, William L. Simon
Year: 2005
Description: Follow-up to Art of Deception with more real-world penetration
testing stories. Shows social engineering tactics used in actual breaches.
Where to find: https://repo.zenk-security.com/Magazine%20E-book/Kevin_Mitnick_-_The_Art_of_Intrusion.pdf

BOOK 16: "Ghost in the Clouds: A Practical Guide to Social Engineering"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Various (search for current texts)
Description: Modern social engineering techniques including OSINT, pretexting,
phone-based attacks (vishing), email phishing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Program to enhance human-machine interaction by modeling human
cognitive processes. Developed tools to measure and augment cognitive
performance.

RESEARCH PROGRAM 2: Targeted Neuroplasticity Training (TNT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: DARPA program to accelerate military training through

learning and memory formation.

virtual settings: emotion manipulation, cybersickness induction, confusion/anxiety
induction.

CONCEPT 1: Reflexive Control (Рефлексивное управление)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Soviet/Russian psychological warfare doctrine. Method of
influencing an opponent's decisions by feeding them selective information
to make them act in ways beneficial to you. Used extensively in modern
information warfare.
Source: https://www.psychologytoday.com/sg/blog/political-animals-and-animal-spirits/202203/maskirovka-the-weaponizing-misinformation

CONCEPT 2: Maskirovka (Маскировка - "Masking/Deception")
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Description: Soviet doctrine of strategic deception. Applied not just to
physical deception but to information operations and psychological
manipulation.

SECTION 7: PROPAGANDA & PUBLIC OPINION MANIPULATION
================================================================================

BOOK 17: "Crystallizing Public Opinion"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Edward L. Bernays
Year: 1923
Description: FOUNDATIONAL PROPAGANDA TEXT. Bernays (Freud's nephew) essentially
invented public relations. This was the first book to define and explain
public relations as a discipline. Introduced the concept that public opinion
can be engineered and shaped systematically.
Where to find:
- https://dokumen.pub/download/crystallizing-public-opinion-9781497698802-9781322989198-1322989192-1497698804.html
Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crystallizing_Public_Opinion

BOOK 18: "Propaganda"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Edward L. Bernays
Year: 1928
Description: Follow-up to Crystallizing Public Opinion. Bernays openly discusses
how elite groups can manipulate public opinion. Introduced the concept of
"the engineering of consent" - treating public opinion as a commodity to
be shaped for commercial/governmental purposes.
Where to find: Search "Bernays Propaganda PDF" - public domain in some jurisdictions

BOOK 19: "Propaganda: The Public Mind and Its Enemies"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Author: Noam Chomsky (more academic analysis)
Description: Critical analysis of Western propaganda systems and mass
media manipulation.
