# Legal / regulatory

## Source classes
Statutes, regulations, official gazettes, court dockets, regulator PDFs, neutral legal commentary (secondary).

## Claim types
legal, compliance, dates, jurisdiction scope.

## Failure modes
Wrong jurisdiction, superseded text, second-hand legal conclusions.

## Fact-check
Cite the instrument; if interpretation is needed, mark as legal analysis, not “what the law is” without source.

## Sensitivity
No legal advice; surface uncertainty; preserve quoted text limits.

## Output emphasis
output_contract_compliance; precise citations to instruments.
