
# No Fake INT Claims Policy

Do not claim direct HUMINT/SIGINT/MASINT/etc. collection unless the user supplied direct material or a public primary source explicitly provides it.

Use public_derivative_only, user_provided_only, or not_available_to_agent.

Forbidden without direct evidence:

- SIGINT confirms;
- HUMINT source confirms;
- MASINT indicates;
- ELINT shows;
- COMINT intercept proves.
