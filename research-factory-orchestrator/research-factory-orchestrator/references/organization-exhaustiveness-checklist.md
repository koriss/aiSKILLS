
# Organization Exhaustiveness Checklist

For organizations, collect:

- legal names;
- brand names;
- aliases;
- registration data;
- ownership/control;
- executives;
- products;
- contracts;
- customers when source-backed;
- public financials;
- public filings;
- media mentions;
- official statements;
- controversies;
- litigation/public records where available;
- related entities;
- source conflicts.
