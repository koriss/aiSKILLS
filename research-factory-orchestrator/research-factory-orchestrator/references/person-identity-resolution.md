
# Person Identity Resolution

For person research, every social/profile source is first a candidate.

Name match is not identity proof.

Identity statuses:

- confirmed;
- probable;
- candidate;
- rejected;
- unrelated.

Confirmed/probable requires:

- at least one hard signal;
- no critical conflict;
- evidence anchor;
- identity-resolution decision.

Candidate/rejected profiles cannot support factual claims.
