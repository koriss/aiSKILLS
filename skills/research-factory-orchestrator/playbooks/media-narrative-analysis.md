# Media / narrative analysis

## Source classes
Primary media, official corrections, data-first outlets; separate reporting vs commentary.

## Claim types
attribution, quotes, what happened vs interpretation.

## Failure modes
Headline re-reporting, missing corrections, out-of-context clips.

## Fact-check
Verify quotes against originals; timecodes where relevant.

## Sensitivity
Defamation, minors — avoid harm; stick to public evidence.

## Output emphasis
citation_faithfulness for quotes; separate facts vs frames.
