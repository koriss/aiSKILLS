
# Information Integrity Classification

Classify observed material as:

- official_claim;
- reported_claim;
- opinion;
- unsupported_claim;
- false_or_contradicted_claim;
- misinformation_possible;
- disinformation_possible;
- malinformation_possible;
- propaganda_indicator;
- satire_or_parody;
- unknown.

Do not assert intent unless evidence supports it.
