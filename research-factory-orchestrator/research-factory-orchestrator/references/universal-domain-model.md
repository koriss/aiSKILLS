# Universal Domain Model

The same process applies to any topic.

Examples:
- Game research: reviews, developer notes, patch notes, player data, sales, interviews, technical analysis.
- Politics: official statements, laws, voting records, policy docs, reputable reporting, public data.
- Biology essay: textbooks, review papers, primary literature, educational sources, diagrams/data.
- Business: filings, registries, financial reports, market data.
- History: primary documents, archives, scholarly sources.
- Technology: documentation, standards, repos, changelogs, advisories.

These are examples, not hardcoded recipes.
