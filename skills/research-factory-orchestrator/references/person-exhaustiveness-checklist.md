
# Person Exhaustiveness Checklist

For person research, collect public source-backed categories:

- identity variants;
- aliases;
- name changes;
- professional roles;
- organizations;
- business links;
- public registries;
- publications;
- interviews;
- conference appearances;
- social profiles;
- court/public legal records where available;
- media mentions;
- academic traces;
- technical work;
- public contact channels;
- geography as sources provide it;
- timeline;
- associates/organizations when source-backed;
- same-name candidates;
- rejected candidates;
- contradictions;
- unverified candidates.
