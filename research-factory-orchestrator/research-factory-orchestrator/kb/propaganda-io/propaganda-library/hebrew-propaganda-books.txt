## 1. ROBERT CIALDINI - "השפעה: פסיכולוגיה של השכנוע"

**Title:** השפעה: פסיכולוגיה של השכנוע (Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion)
**Author:** רוברט ב' צ'לדיני (Robert B. Cialdini)
**Year:** 1984 (original), Hebrew translation published in Israel
**Description:** The classic textbook on persuasion - 6 universal principles: reciprocation, consistency, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity, and unity. Translated into Hebrew and sold in Israel via Matar Books. This is one of the most widely available Hebrew resources on influence and persuasion.
**URL:** https://www.matarbooks.co.il/%D7%9E%D7%95%D7%A6%D7%A8%D7%99%D7%9D/%D7%94%D7%A9%D7%A4%D7%A2%D7%94

## 2. "המערכה על התודעה" - Israeli Intelligence Research Institute

**Title:** המערכה על התודעה (The Campaign for Consciousness / Mind Battle)
**Author:** המכון לחקר המתודולוגיה של המודיעין (Institute for Intelligence Research Methodology)
**Year:** Published in IDF intelligence journal "מודיעין הלכה ומעשה"
**Description:** MAJOR Hebrew academic work on psychological warfare, propaganda, information operations, manipulation, persuasion, strategic communication, PSYOPS, and influence campaigns. Defines concepts: manipulation (מניפולציה) as a type of influence using deception, propaganda, psychological warfare vs. information warfare. Authoritative Israeli intelligence source.
**URL:** https://www.intelligence-research.org.il/userfiles/banners/Gilayon%20-%204.pdf
**FILE_PATH:** (not downloaded)

## NOTES

### Search Summary
- **Hebrew Cialdini (השפעה):** Confirmed available in Hebrew, widely sold in Israel via Matar Books. Also found on dokumen.pub with Hebrew title "השפעה: תיאוריה ופרקטיקה"
- **Bernays (תעמולה / Propaganda):** No confirmed Hebrew translation found in searches
- **Lifton (brainwashing):** No confirmed Hebrew translation found in searches
- **Israeli PSYOPS / הלחמה פסיכולוגית:** Strong Israeli academic literature found, especially from IDF intelligence journal "מודיעין הלכה ומעשה" and INSS
- **מניפולציה (manipulation):** Hebrew academic papers exist, especially "המערכה על התודעה" which explicitly analyzes manipulation as a form of influence using deception

### Key Sources Identified
1. **המכון לחקר המתודולוגיה של המודיעין** (intelligence-research.org.il) - BEST single source for Hebrew PSYOPS/propaganda theory
2. **INSS** (inss.org.il) - Israeli defense/national security studies, influence operations
3. **צה"ל / IDF** (idf.il) - Official military doctrine and articles
4. **מכללת ספיר / Sapir College** (mediaframes.sapir.ac.il) - Communication and media studies
5. **Tel Aviv University / DCIR** - Academic PSYOPS papers
6. **Israel Democracy Institute** (idi.org.il) - Democracy and technology/persuasion
7. **המכון הישראלי לדמוקרטיה** - AI and manipulation/persuasion

### Limitations
- Most Hebrew "textbooks" on this topic are actually academic papers or journal articles rather than traditional textbooks
- No confirmed Hebrew translations of Edward Bernays or Robert Lifton found
- Hebrew academic literature on this topic is largely post-2000 and heavily focused on terrorism/Israel context
