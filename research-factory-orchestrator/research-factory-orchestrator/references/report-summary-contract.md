
# Report Summary Contract

Every full report must contain three summary layers:

1. Preview summary
2. Executive summary
3. Section summaries

Executive summary fields:

```text
verdict
key_findings
key_numbers
confidence
caveats
why_it_matters
next_steps
```

Rules:

```text
Summary must not introduce facts absent from verified/allowed claims.
Summary must separate facts from interpretation.
Low/unknown confidence claims must not be presented as established facts.
```
