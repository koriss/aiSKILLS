# Market research

## Source classes
Trade statistics where primary, company filings, industry reports (flag methodology), reputable surveys (method note).

## Claim types
market size, share, growth, competitive mapping.

## Failure modes
Top-down TAM with no method; cherry-picked estimates; currency/date mixing.

## Fact-check
Single-estimate → confidence cap; mark assumptions.

## Sensitivity
Forward-looking = hypothesis.

## Output emphasis
completeness of scenarios; source_quality.
