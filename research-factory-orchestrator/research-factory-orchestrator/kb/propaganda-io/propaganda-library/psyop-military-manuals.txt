## 1. US ARMY PSYCHOLOGICAL OPERATIONS DOCTRINE

### FM 3-05.30 Psychological Operations
- **Title:** Field Manual FM 3-05.30 (MCRP 3-40.6) — Psychological Operations
- **Author/Organization:** US Army / Marine Corps
- **Year:** 2005 (April 15)
- **Description:** The keystone publication for PSYOP principles. Covers the employment of psychological operations as a tool of national policy. Details the five PSYOP mission types and seven PSYOP functions. Essential foundational manual for understanding US military psychological operations doctrine.
- **URL:** https://www.scribd.com/document/224477113/Field-Manual-FM-3-05-30-Psychological-Operation-MCRP-3-40-6-April-15-2005
- **URL:** https://apps.dtic.mil/sti/tr/pdf/ADA432676.pdf
- **File Path:** (PDF available via DTIC and archive sources)

### FM 3-05.301 Psychological Operations Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures
- **Title:** FM 3-05.301 — Psychological Operations Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures
- **Author/Organization:** US Army
- **Year:** 2003 (December)
- **Description:** Describes procedures and provides templates for conducting the five PSYOP missions and seven PSYOP functions in a systematic, chronological fashion. Details operational-level PSYOP planning and execution.
- **URL:** https://archive.org/details/Fm305301PsychologicalOperationsTacticsTechniquesAndProcedures
- **URL:** https://info.publicintelligence.net/USArmy-PsyOpsTactics.pdf
- **URL:** http://www.communicationcache.com/uploads/1/0/8/8/10887248/psychological_operations_tactics_techniques_and_procedures_-_2003.pdf
- **URL:** https://www.bits.de/NRANEU/others/amd-us-archive/fm3-05-301%2803%29.pdf

### FM 3-05.302 Tactical Psychological Operations
- **Title:** FM 3-05.302 — Tactical Psychological Operations Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures
- **Author/Organization:** US Army
- **Year:** 2005
- **Description:** Tactical-level PSYOP manual covering tactical psychological operations procedures. Describes the command and control structure for tactical PSYOP units.
- **URL:** https://archive.org/details/Fm305302TacticalPsychologicalOperationsTacticsTechniquesAndProcedures
- **URL:** https://www.scribd.com/doc/127025030/FM-3-05-302-Tactical-Psychological-Operations-Tactics-Techniques-And-Procedures

### FM 2-0 Intelligence Operations
- **Title:** FM 2-0 — Intelligence Operations
- **Author/Organization:** US Army
- **Year:** 2004 (May), also 2009 edition
- **Description:** The Army's keystone manual for military intelligence (MI) doctrine. Covers intelligence fundamentals, battlefield intelligence, and intelligence support to operations.
- **URL:** https://archive.org/details/milmanual-fm-2-0-intelligence
- **URL:** https://armypubs.army.mil/epubs/DR_pubs/DR_a/ARN39259-FM_2-0-000-WEB-2.pdf

## 4. BLACK / WHITE / GREY PROPAGANDA CLASSIFICATION

### White, Grey, and Black Propaganda Classification
- **Title:** US RADIO: Propaganda and Espionage
- **Author/Organization:** CIA
- **Year:** 1945-1950s era
- **Description:** CIA classification system for propaganda types:
  - WHITE: Disseminated and acknowledged by the sponsor or accredited agency
  - GREY: Does not clearly identify the sponsor
  - BLACK: Falsely attributed to a source other than the actual sponsor
- **URL:** https://www.cia.gov/readingroom/docs/CIA-RDP75-00001R000100020025-8.pdf

### Propaganda Classification — Powerbase
- **Title:** Propaganda — White, Grey, Black
- **Author/Organization:** Powerbase
- **Year:** 2015
- **Description:** Detailed breakdown of propaganda classification:
  - White: Source truthfully identified, not strongly biased
  - Grey: No clear indication of origin, or origin attributed to ally
  - Black: Origin attributed to a source other than actual sponsor
- **URL:** https://powerbase.info/index.php/Propaganda

### Backfires: White, Black and Grey — Academic Paper
- **Title:** Backfires: White, Black and Grey
- **Author/Organization:** University of Wollongong
- **Year:** 2007
- **Description:** Academic analysis of the three-tier propaganda classification system. Discusses the use of backfires (false flag propaganda) as a specific black propaganda technique.
- **URL:** https://documents.uow.edu.au/~bmartin/pubs/07jiw.php

- **Title:** The Propaganda Technique in the World War
- **Author/Organization:** Harold D. Lasswell
