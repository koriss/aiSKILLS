============================================================
NOTES
============================================================

Many Soviet-era textbooks are available through:
1. Western archival research (CIA, DTIC declassifications)
2. Archive.org digitization of library collections
3. Academic repositories in Eastern Europe
4. BannedThought.net for Soviet/MLM texts
5. Marxists Internet Archive for primary Leninist sources

Direct Soviet-era PDFs (original printing) are harder to find as they were 
typically classified or limited to party/military distribution. Most available
materials are either: (a) Western analytical studies, (b) declassified Western
intel documents, or (c) later reprints hosted on archive sites.
