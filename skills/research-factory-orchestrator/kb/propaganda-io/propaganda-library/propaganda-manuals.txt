### 1.1 "Propaganda" by Edward L. Bernays (1928)

- **Author:** Edward L. Bernays (the "father of public relations")
- **Year:** 1928 (reissued 1936)
- **Description:** The foundational text on modern propaganda and public relations. 
  Bernays was nephew of Freud and pioneered psychological manipulation techniques 
  for controlling public opinion. This book coined the term "engineering of 
  consent" and laid theoretical groundwork for modern PR industry.
- **Where to find online:**
  - Archive.org: https://archive.org/details/bernays-edward-l.-propaganda-1928-1936_202107

## SECTION 2: MODERN PROPAGANDA & MANIPULATION THEORY

### 2.1 "Manufacturing Consent: The Political Economy of the Mass Media" 
     by Edward S. Herman & Noam Chomsky

- **Author:** Edward S. Herman & Noam Chomsky
- **Year:** 1988
- **Description:** Seminal work on media propaganda model. Introduces concept 
  of "manufacturing consent" - how corporate media systems filter news to serve 
  elite interests. Five filters model: ownership, advertising, sourcing, 
  flak, and anti-communist ideology.
- **Where to find online:**
  - Archive.org (full PDF): https://archive.org/download/pdfy-NekqfnoWIEuYgdZl/Manufacturing%20Consent%20%5BThe%20Political%20Economy%20Of%20The%20Mass%20Media%5D.pdf

### 2.2 "Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion" by Robert B. Cialdini

- **Author:** Robert B. Cialdini (Arizona State University)
- **Year:** 1984 (revised editions 2006, 2021)
- **Description:** Scientific analysis of six principles of persuasion: 
  reciprocity, commitment/consistency, social proof, authority, liking, 
  scarcity. Evidence-based research on manipulation techniques used in 
  marketing, politics, and propaganda.
- **Where to find online:**
  - Archive.org: https://ia800203.us.archive.org/33/items/ThePsychologyOfPersuasion/The%20Psychology%20of%20Persuasion.pdf
  - PDF Coffee: https://pdfcoffee.com/download/influence-the-psychology-of-persuasion-collins-business-essentials-robert-b-cialdini-phd-pdf-free.html
  - Various summary versions available
- **Local file:** Not yet downloaded
