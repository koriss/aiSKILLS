# Analytical Memo Writing Policy

Analytical reports must behave like decision-support memos, not prose dumps.

Required analytic qualities:

```text
clear bottom line
key judgments separated from facts
source quality and credibility described
uncertainty explained
assumptions made explicit
alternatives/contradictions considered
customer relevance / why it matters addressed
logical argumentation
```

Report structure should use:

```text
BLUF / verdict
key judgments
what we know
what we infer
what remains uncertain
source/evidence base
implications
next checks
```

Estimative language must separate fact from judgment:

```text
confirmed fact
reported claim
self-claim
assessment
likely / probably / almost certainly
unknown / cannot assess
```
