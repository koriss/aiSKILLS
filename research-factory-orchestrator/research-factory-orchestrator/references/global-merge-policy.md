
# Global Merge Policy

Shard outputs are not final.

Required flow:

work-unit outputs → normalized shard artifacts → global merge → contradiction/gap pass → final report.

Global merge artifacts include:

- global-sources.json;
- global-claims.json;
- global-evidence-cards.json;
- global-citation-map.json;
- global-coverage-matrix.json;
- merge-conflicts.json.
