
# Report Structure Policy

Separate:

```text
facts
data
evidence
analysis
uncertainty
actions
```

Required universal sections:

```text
Header
Executive Summary
Scope & Method
Key Findings
Evidence & Facts
Structured Data
Contradictions / Gaps
Implications / Risks
Sources
Appendices
```

Sections may be marked `not_applicable_with_reason`, but not silently omitted.
