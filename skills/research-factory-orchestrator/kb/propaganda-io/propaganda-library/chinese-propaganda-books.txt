## 3. 舆论战 (PUBLIC OPINION WARFARE)

---

TITLE: 舆论战 (Public Opinion Warfare)
AUTHOR: 朱金平
YEAR: 2005
DESCRIPTION: China's FIRST dedicated monograph on public opinion warfare. Systematic study of舆论战 as a military strategy. Covers: concept and history of public opinion warfare, its characteristics, role as military force multiplier, information carriers, material foundations, relationship with親緣戰術. States "舆论战开展于武力对抗之前，贯穿于武力对抗之中，继续于武力对抗之后" (Public opinion warfare starts before armed conflict, continues through it, and follows after). Published by 中国言实出版社. 291 pages.
URL: https://www.shukui.net/book/2447140.html

## SUMMARY NOTES

- Most documents found are PDFs hosted on Chinese ebook/library sites
- Direct download links often redirect through file sharing services (Pan.baidu, Quark, etc.)
- 朱金平's 舆论战 (2005) is the most directly relevant academic text on propaganda-as-warfare
- 军事心理学 provides academic grounding in psychological operations
- Sun Tzu translations with PLA-relevant commentary (郭化若) are most militarily applicable
- Beijing/Shanghai research centers primarily produce journals rather than publicly downloadable books
- 认知战 as a distinct PRC term is more common in post-2018 Taiwan defense literature
