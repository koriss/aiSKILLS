# Science / health

## Source classes
Peer-reviewed, official health agencies, preprints labeled as such, clinical registries with caveats.

## Claim types
medical, biological mechanism, study outcomes, safety.

## Failure modes
Preprint = proven; population mismatch; p-hacked headlines.

## Fact-check
Study design, N, endpoint, follow-up, conflicts of interest when available.

## Sensitivity
Medical advice; include “not personal medical advice” in limitations for consumer contexts.

## Output emphasis
conservative language for causal claims; contradictions.
