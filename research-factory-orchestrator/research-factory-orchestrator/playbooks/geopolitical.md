# Geopolitical

## Source classes
Government releases, multilateral org primary pages, major wires (secondary), think-tank methods papers.

## Claim types
location, status, attribution, legal/regulatory, historical timeline.

## Failure modes
Narrative-only sources, single-origin amplification, map/geodata misread.

## Fact-check
Separately label facts vs analysis; require provenance for numbers and boundaries.

## Sensitivity
Disinformation and sanctions topics; avoid speculative escalation language without labels.

## Output emphasis
conflict_contradiction_handling, source independence.
