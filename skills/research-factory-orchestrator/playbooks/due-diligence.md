# Due diligence

## Source classes
Registries, filings, liens (where available), contract excerpts user provides, direct interviews only if in scope.

## Claim types
ownership, risk, financial, legal encumbrance, operations.

## Failure modes
Missing negative evidence; single-source “clean bill of health.”

## Fact-check
Red-flag pass; independence of corroboration; data gaps in limitations.

## Sensitivity
Insider / MNPI; do not exfiltrate private data; respect NDA.

## Output emphasis
resume_readiness; explicit unknowns; safety_compliance.
