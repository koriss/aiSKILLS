# Corporate finance

## Source classes
Filings, registries, official exchanges, company primary releases.

## Claim types
ownership, financial, legal, dates, market metrics.

## Failure modes
Press-release-only chains, pro forma confusion, TTM mix-ups.

## Fact-check
Reconcile numbers to table/line; footnote restatements.

## Sensitivity
MNPI — do not infer non-public data.

## Output emphasis
citation_accuracy for numeric claims.
